A Simple Embark

A simple embark

Sharp like a single tooth of a shark

You press it against your skin

No disgrace is shown

But ready

To feel on what we call

Pain


How many times will it strike?

The problem is

You continue to like

Like the feeling of constraint


What will be done now?

Just take the jacket

Pull the sleeves down to the tip

To heave what was done

I wouldn't call it fun

But

You insisted

The job was done


9/13/11 RW
