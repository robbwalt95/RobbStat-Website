An eye for an eye - Only Blinds

You are blind and I can see.

Your jealousy makes me heave.

You think after getting even, you're simply out? Just leavin'?

Sorry to say, but it's too true to believe in.

Your pain has grown as mine diminished.

Neither of what we feel will ever be finished.

Take your weapon, deal me dead if you find it just.

But know what you feel will only rust.

Learn from my mistakes and stop these earthquakes.

Else I promise you:
Our eyes combined prove we deserve to be blind.


12/4/21 RW
